There are some brief details of my working modules In Goodluck

GOODLUCK India Ltd. Manufacture CDW and ERW Steel Tube. Complete manufacturing process is managed with the help of the Microsoft Dynamics NAV.

Modules

Sales:


It is an important and essential function for an organization. Sales handle all the activities for domestic and export sales of an organization.
Important features of Sales:
 • Check the price of goods and services like rebate or discount, which is given to a customer.
• Check the availability of a product in stock.
• Help to Generating bills or invoices.
• Determine the details of materials based on conditions.
• A simple credit check and automatic credit check for managing the credit limits of the customers.

Purchase:


Important features of Purchase:
 • Check the price of goods and services like rebate or discount, which is given by the Vendors.
• Check the Requirement of the Product.
• Help to Generating bills or invoices.
• Determine the details of materials based on conditions.

Manufacturing:


Important features of Manufacturing:
 • Create the Item, Production BOM and Families.
• Create Release Production Order for Production of an Item.
• Machine Centre and Work Centre.

Financial Management:


Important features of Financial Management:
 • Posting the General Ledger Entries.
• Posting the Bank Receipt Voucher.
• Posting the Bank Payment Voucher.
